The Competition

Tournament Format

Ryder Cup–style team competition across four competitive rounds. All formats use own ball — every player plays every shot. Two teams of four battle for Chandra Cup glory.

Friday Morning · Karoo
Round 1
Four-Ball (Best Ball)
Two-man teams play their own ball throughout. The lower score of the pair counts on each hole. Fast, strategic, and forgiving — the perfect opener on Karoo's dramatic layout.
2 matches · 2 points available
Friday Afternoon · The Squeeze
Round 2
Four-Ball (Best Ball)
Same format, different pairings. The 10-hole Squeeze makes every point count — there's no room to coast. Captains redraw partnerships to shuffle the lineup.
2 matches · 2 points available
Saturday · Roost + Wedge
Round 3
Mixed Four-Ball
Morning best-ball on the Roost, followed by an afternoon session on the Wedge par-3 course. The Wedge session counts as its own mini-match — par-3 drama is real.
4 matches · 4 points available
